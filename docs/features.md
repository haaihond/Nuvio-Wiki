## Nuvio's settings can be overhwelming. Here is a comprehensive breakdown of Nuvio's features:

**UI, Customization & Organization 🎨**
1. Multiple Profiles: Nuvio supports individual user profiles, allowing different members of a household to keep their watch history, custom catalogs, and recommendations completely separate.
2. Home Screen Management: You have total control over your dashboard. You can reorder, toggle, or completely disable default and custom catalogs so you only see exactly what you care about.
3. Custom Layouts: The app offers multiple UI layouts, allowing you to tweak how posters, rows, and media details are presented (e.g., choosing between dense grids or large, cinematic scrolling rows).
4. Collections & Custom Catalogs: Beyond standard addon scraping, Nuvio lets you build deep custom collections. You can group media by streaming platforms, studios, specific genres, or directors, and the app will automatically apply custom background artwork and logos to make these rows look native.
